About Andrew

Andrew is a multidisciplinary graphic designer with a sharp focus on vector systems and print production. He has deep experience with CorelDRAW, advanced vector techniques, typography, and pre-press workflows. Over the last decade Andrew has expanded his practice to include front-end development — making designs that translate seamlessly to web and interactive media.

Select Achievements

  • Lead designer for award-winning packaging for a regional beverage brand (2023).
  • Built a reusable SVG icon system used across three startups.
  • Designed and implemented a responsive marketing microsite with pixel-perfect assets.

Approach

Andrew follows a systems-driven workflow: research → sketch → vector prototype → responsive spec → export & handoff. He favors clear typography, purposeful color, and vector-first assets that are easy to scale and integrate with developers' tooling.
